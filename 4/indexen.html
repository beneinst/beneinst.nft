Author

On this page, you can get to know Gerardo D’Orrico better, the author behind the works you have enjoyed on Beneinst. Here, you will find a brief biography, where you can learn about his experiences, passions, and both past and future publications.

Brief Biography

Gerardo D'Orrico is a writer and the creator of Beneinst, born in Cosenza on March 6, 1976. After completing his secondary education, he attended the universities of Arcavacata and Bologna, but did not earn a degree. However, he acquired solid knowledge in computer science and some musical instruments. His youth was spent between his home in Luzzi and Cosenza for his studies, or in his mother’s hometown of Villapiana by the sea. He traveled a lot, and after military service, he helped his father with work and nurtured his passion for writing prose, along with computer science and software programming. He currently lives in Luzzi, where he dedicates himself to writing, revising, and publishing his works, including four diary-style books: "Good and Evil, Memories," "A Ceiling of Ash," "We Are Already Ourselves in Ten Minutes," and "Say It Yourself."

Mission

Our mission is to create a free, open, and inclusive platform where everyone can express themselves freely and share their ideas, opinions, and creativity without restrictions or censorship. We believe that free access to knowledge and culture is essential for the development of a fairer and more compassionate society. In addition, we aim to promote the value of art and culture by providing a space for the publication and dissemination of creative works, including writings, poems, drawings, paintings, and photographs. Here, you can also interact with other users, comment on their content, discover new sources of inspiration, or use the storage space for your works and access them wherever you are. We are committed to keeping the site always updated, secure, and easily accessible to all users.
